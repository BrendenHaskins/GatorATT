Group Agreements

  • Team members agree to communicate openly and promptly, fostering an environment where questions and concerns are addressed in a timely manner
  • Team members commit to respecting each other's time and workload, ensuring that requests for collaboration or assistance are made with consideration to individual priorities and deadlines
  • Decisions within the team will be made collaboratively, with all members encouraged to contribute their insights and perspectives to achieve well-informed and inclusive outcomes
  • Team agrees to hold regular meetings every Tuesday and Thursday at 11 am to discuss project progress, address challenges, and synchronize efforts. Attendance is mandatory unless communicated in advance with valid reasons.
